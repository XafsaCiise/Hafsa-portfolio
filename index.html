Who I Am

Data meets purpose.

I'm a Computer Science and Information Technology professional who genuinely loves the moment messy data becomes something useful. With a Bachelor's in Computer Science and a Master's in IT, I've worked across education, government, and private sector environments — and what's stayed constant is my curiosity about how information flows and how to make it work better for the people who depend on it.

My work spans database architecture, SQL reporting, data validation, and operational dashboards. I've built systems from scratch and walked into broken ones — both taught me a lot. I also care deeply about the human side: training teams, building capacity, and making data accessible to people who don't call themselves data people.
